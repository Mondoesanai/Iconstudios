Founder & Director

Normita
Joven

Icon Studios Universal

Normita Joven is the visionary founder and director of Icon Studios Universal, one of the most respected talent agencies in the Dallas–Fort Worth area. With over 14 years in the talent and entertainment industry, she has built an agency rooted in authenticity, genuine relationships, and an unwavering commitment to helping artists live their dreams.

Under her leadership, Icon Studios Universal has placed talent in film, television, commercials, print campaigns, billboards, fashion shows, and corporate events — from local DFW productions to nationally recognized Fortune 500 campaigns.

Normita's philosophy is simple: every talent deserves to be seen, heard, and placed in rooms where their gifts can shine. That belief has defined ICU's culture from day one.

Talent Agent

Nick
Callaway

Men's & Specialty Division

Nick Callaway manages Icon Studios Universal's men's and specialty talent roster, bringing a strategic and relationship-driven approach to talent placement. His extensive network spans casting directors, production houses, commercial brands, and independent filmmakers across Texas and nationwide.

Nick's talent for identifying potential and pairing it with the right opportunity has resulted in dozens of successful placements in leading national commercial campaigns, film and television roles, and high-profile print editorial work.

His background in talent development means he understands the full arc of a career — and brings that long-view perspective to every artist he represents.

Chanel
Bracy

Women's, Teens & Kids Division

Chanel Bracy leads Icon Studios Universal's women's, teen, and kids divisions with a hands-on, people-first approach to talent development and career guidance. Her sharp eye for emerging talent has launched the careers of numerous artists across modeling, acting, and commercial work.

Chanel's strength lies in the way she connects with talent personally — understanding their goals, strengths, and aspirations before matching them with the opportunities that will move them forward. Her approach has resulted in a high rate of repeat bookings and long-term client relationships.

She brings warmth, professionalism, and a deep passion for the industry to everything she does at ICU.
